Intro to Tokyo

Tokyo, origionally called Edo, has been the capital of Japan since 1603. It is the largest, safest, and most technologically developed city in the world. It is also the birthplace of karaoke, nintendo, and some of your favorite cartoon characters.

There is plenty to explore in tokyo, from the modern, bustling parts of the city, to the more remote, scenic, and historical areas. Here some things you can do in both.

Modern Tokyo

  • Arcades
  • Theme parks
  • Karaoke

Scenic Tokyo

  • Mt. Fuji
  • Senso-ji Shrine
  • Ueno Park
